Frequently Asked Questions

What does SVG compression do? +
SVG compression removes unnecessary metadata, empty elements, editor-specific attributes (Illustrator, Figma, Sketch, Inkscape), and redundant code without affecting how the SVG looks or functions.
Are my files uploaded to a server? +
No. Everything runs in your browser using WebAssembly. Your files never leave your device. No uploads, no servers, no privacy concerns.
How much smaller will my SVGs get? +
Typical savings range from 20% to 70%. SVGs from design tools contain extensive metadata that our optimizer cleanly removes.
